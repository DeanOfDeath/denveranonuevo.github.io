WHO
AM I?

“

I am Denver Anonuevo. My journey in technology began with a passion for understanding how systems work and how data can drive meaningful solutions. As a BS Information Technology graduate majoring in Business Analytics from Don Mariano Marcos Memorial State University, I graduated Cum Laude, reflecting my dedication to academic excellence and continuous learning.

Throughout my academic journey, I developed skills in programming languages like Python, R, SQL, and JavaScript, while also learning data analytics tools such as Power BI, Tableau, and Looker Studio. My skills extends to web development using HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, and PHP, allowing me to create responsive and dynamic web applications. My coursework in Statistical Analysis, Database Management, Business Intelligence, Data Mining, and Machine Learning provided me with a strong foundation in both technical and analytical thinking.

My interests span across various areas of technology, from web development and database management to exploring machine learning concepts. Through academic projects like Terraturtle, I've had the opportunity to work with predictive modeling for environmental applications. I enjoy working with cloud platforms and creating data visualizations that help tell meaningful stories. With my commitment to continuous learning, I'm excited to contribute to technology solutions that can make a positive difference.
